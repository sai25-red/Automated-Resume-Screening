Automated resume screening using natural language processing

Resume Screening is the process of evaluating the resume of the job seekers based on a specific requirement. It is used to identify the candidate eligibility for a job by matching all the requirements needed for the offered role with their resume information such as education qualification, skill sets, technical stuff etc.
